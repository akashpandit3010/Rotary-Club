Serving with Heart, Transforming Lives Welcome

Welcome to the Rotary Club of Talegaon MIDC! Our club is committed to serving our community through impactful initiatives, fostering unity, and creating lasting change. With a diverse membership dedicated to the values of Rotary, we are a driving force for progress in Talegaon and beyond.

ABOUT US

The Rotary Club of Talegaon MIDC is part of Rotary International, a global network of neighbors, friends, leaders, and problem-solvers who come together to make positive, lasting change in communities around the world. Since our establishment, we’ve been dedicated to bringing people together for the common good, whether through local service projects, international initiatives, or providing a platform for professional development.

Mission Statement
"To provide service to others, promote integrity, and advance world understanding, goodwill, and peace through our fellowship of business, professional, and community leaders."

Our Vision
"To create a world where people unite and take action to create lasting change—across the globe, in our communities, and in ourselves."
